The Snowbird Taco Tour

Heading 1


Our group plans to use Yelp data found on Kaggle in order to analyze taco and burrito locations from the US and Canada. Our inspiration is rooted in the deep and unabiding love nearly all Americans have for the Mexican (or maybe not-so-Mexican depending on how close you are to Canada) favorite. We felt like this was a fun topic because of its relatability, and also we could put a really spicy twist on our dashboards.
