The Snowbird Taco Tour


Welcome to the Snowbird Taco Tour home page! The Snowbird Taco Tour was created by four students from the University of Minnesota’s Data Analysis and Visualization bootcamp. We were inspired by the deep and unabiding love nearly all Americans have for the Mexican (or maybe not-so-Mexican depending on how close you are to Canada) favorite. The project explores a Kaggle’s data set based on the yelp API. During our exploratory data analysis we started to find the limitations of our data which was the incompleteness of the data set. We found the state with the most data in our data set was Arizona. Every winter many Minnesotan’s head south and make Arizona their winter home. Thus the “Snowbird Taco Tour” was born.
